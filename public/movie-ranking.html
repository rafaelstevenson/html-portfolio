The Best Movies According to Rafael

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Now You See Me

This movie is so magical. It talks about magic and it actually inspired me how illusion and magic tricks are so cool and made me wanting to learn magic tricks last time. hahaha

Sherlock Holmes BBC

I've always liked mystery stories and this movie just gives the perfect mystery story by the thrill, good music, and cinematography. Love Sherlock!

Queen's Gambit

This movie tells about a story of a genius girl that struggles with mentality and being a chess genius in the period where chess was only typical for males. Fun to watch the main character Beth Harmon as a woman chess genius. Great cinematography and soundtrack by the way!
